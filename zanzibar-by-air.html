Stone Town Tour

Destination - Zanzibar Stone town
Choice of Accomodation Zanzibar Serena Inn Bluebay Beach Resort Tembo Hotel Karafuu Hotel Beach Resort Mapenzi Beach Hotel Breezes Beach Club Stone Town tour.

After breakfast, proceed for a tour to the famous spice plantations in Zanzibar, it's sole producer of spice in the world in large quantities and also a visit to Persians Baths built by the ruler in 1850 known as Sayyid Said.

1

Day 1: Arusha - Marangu - Mandara Hut

Depart Arusha from your base hotel and drive for 2hrs to Marangu . Trek to Mandara for 7 kms, 4-5 hrs, Ascent nOm, Alt: 270Om . Dinner and overnight stay at Mandara Hut (B,L,D).

2

Day 2: Mandara Hut - Horombo

Starting early in the morning towards Horombo Hut you walk 5 hours, to cover the first 4 km or so, the dense rain forest now gives way to the rolling moorland clothed in knee- high gross tussocks dotted here and there with giant heather trees, some standing up to 9 m high.

Here is perhaps the most pleasant point where the landscape looks nice with flowers etc. , and the sun is refreshingly warm. From here, both Kibo and Mawenzi Peaks are magnificently viewed.

Some animal species are found here, i.e. bush duikers etc. The landscape becomes more rugged and rocky, now you arrive at Horombo Hut and at 3761 m above sea level. Dinner and overnight at the Horombo Hut. You should have warm clothes due to strong winds here.

3

Day 4: Horombo Hut - Kibo Hut

MAer breakfast, continue to Kibo Hut through the euphorbia trees and here high altitude causes difficulty in breathing and tiredness is felt.

The path leading you to Kibo Hut passes the water streams and skirts of Mawezi before opening out the Lunar Landscape of the Saddle. It forms a "U" between MAWENZI and KIBO Peaks.

You will have covered 13 km walking 6 Hours from Horombo Hut, and you will be 4732 m above sea level. You will experience the winter night as cold and icy winds encircle the huts accompanied by frost and snow. Dinner and overnight at Kibo Hut.

4

Day 5: Kibo Hut - Horombo Hut

Wake up early - after midnight for the final attempt on the summit, walk for about 5 hours, the steepest and most difficult part of the route, to GILLMAN'S POINT, at 5681 m above sea level, on the Crater rim. Try again, if your stamina allows, for UHURU Peak, reached in about 2 hours. This is the highest point in AITica. After Signing the ,isitors book, descend to Horombo Hut, for dinner and overnight.

5

Day 6: Horombo Hut - Marangu Gate/ Moshi

After breakfast, descend to the MARANGU back to Moshi. Overnight at Spring lands Hotel.

6

Day 7: Moshi Nairobi

After breakfast depart for Nairobi by shuttle bus arriving Nairobi at around 18.30 hrs.
